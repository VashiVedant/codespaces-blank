Finding Housing

Finding a place to live is an important first step when moving to a new country. There are many options available, including apartments, houses, and shared housing. Consider the following when looking for housing:

  • Location: Consider the location of your housing in relation to your work, school, and other important resources.
  • Cost: Determine your budget and look for housing options that fit within it.
  • Amenities: Think about what amenities you need in your housing, such as a kitchen, laundry facilities, or parking.

Obtaining Healthcare

It's important to have access to healthcare when you move to a new country. Consider the following when looking for healthcare options:

  • Insurance: Check to see if your employer provides health insurance or if you need to purchase it on your own.
  • Doctors: Look for doctors or clinics that are accessible and provide the type of care you need.
  • Prescriptions: If you take medications, make sure to bring a supply with you and refill them as needed.

Enrolling in School

If you or your children need to enroll in school, there are a few steps to follow:

  • Research schools: Look for schools that meet your needs and preferences, such as location, cost, and academic programs.
  • Gather documents: You may need to provide proof of residency, immunization records, and other documents to enroll in school.
  • Enroll: Follow the enrollment process for the school you have chosen, which may include filling out forms, attending an orientation, and paying fees.
